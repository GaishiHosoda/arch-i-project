CC Free5

Yurie Suzuki

Project CC free is inspired by the current peace crisis in progress and my dear friend who study Conflict Resolution at Kings College London. Each artwork presents common human value to our society to be united in a form of artwork. One of the best pieces of input from my bright friend about peacekeeping is that suggesting common values to the communities in conflict can be one way of uniting them than fragmenting them. How might Artists be useful in a real-life crisis has been a question and challenge to me as a researcher and artist. Project CC free reminds us that our common values such as "Peace", "Green", "Friends" are all copyright free gifts that our ancestors left to our society today. Note: All the profits raised from the "CC free" project will be donated to peacekeeping organizations. Further detail of profit usage will be reported on Artist's website.
